Let \(S\) denote a set of vectors in \(\IR^n\) and suppose that \(\vec{u},\vec{v}\in\vspan(S)\text{,}\) \(c\in\IR\) and that \(\vec{w}\in\IR^n\text{.}\) Which of the following vectors might not belong to \(\vspan(S)\text{?}\)
  1. \(\displaystyle \vec{0}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle \vec{u}+\vec{w}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \vec{u}+\vec{v}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle c\vec{u}\)
