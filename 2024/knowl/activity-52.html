Activity 2.3.10.

Let \(W=\setBuilder{\left[\begin{array}{c} x \\ y \\ z \end{array}\right]}{ x+2y+z=4}\text{.}\)
(a)
Is \(W\) the empty set?
(b)
Which of these statements is valid?
  1. \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\in W\text{,}\) and \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right]\in W\text{,}\) so \(W\) is a subspace.
  2. \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\in W\text{,}\) and \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right]\in W\text{,}\) so \(W\) is not a subspace.
  3. \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\in W\text{,}\) but \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right]\not\in W\text{,}\) so \(W\) is a subspace.
  4. \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\in W\text{,}\) but \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 2 \\ 2 \end{array}\right]\not\in W\text{,}\) so \(W\) is not a subspace.
(c)
Which of these statements is valid?
  1. \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\in W\text{,}\) and \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right]\in W\text{,}\) so \(W\) is a subspace.
  2. \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\in W\text{,}\) and \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right]\in W\text{,}\) so \(W\) is not a subspace.
  3. \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\in W\text{,}\) but \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right]\not\in W\text{,}\) so \(W\) is a subspace.
  4. \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \\ 1 \end{array}\right]\in W\text{,}\) but \(\left[\begin{array}{c} 0 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array}\right]\not\in W\text{,}\) so \(W\) is not a subspace.
