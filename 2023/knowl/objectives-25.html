Learning Outcomes 5.

How do we understand linear maps geometrically?
By the end of this chapter, you should be able to...
  1. Describe how a row operation affects the determinant of a matrix.
  2. Compute the determinant of a \(4\times 4\) matrix.
  3. Find the eigenvalues of a \(2\times 2\) matrix.
  4. Find a basis for the eigenspace of a \(4\times 4\) matrix associated with a given eigenvalue.
