Remark 2.3.11.

Proofs of an equality \(LEFT=RIGHT\) should generally be of one of two forms:
  1. Using a chain of equalities:
    \begin{align*} LEFT &= \cdots\\ &= \cdots\\ &= \cdots\\ &= RIGHT \end{align*}
  2. When the assumption \(THIS=THAT\) is already known or assumed to be true :
    \begin{align*} && THIS &= THAT\\ & \Rightarrow& \cdots &= \cdots\\ & \Rightarrow& \cdots &= \cdots\\ & \Rightarrow& LEFT &= RIGHT \end{align*}
