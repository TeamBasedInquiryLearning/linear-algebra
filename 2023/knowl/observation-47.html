Observation A.2.10.

\begin{equation*} \vec{x} = \left[\begin{array}{c} 2 \\ 4 \\2 \\ 2.5 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ 1\end{array}\right] \end{equation*}

\begin{equation*} 2, 4, 1, 3, 7, 5, 6 \end{equation*}
