Task 2.5.8.b.
\begin{equation*} \left\{ \left[\begin{array}{c}2\\3\\0\\-1\end{array}\right], \left[\begin{array}{c}2\\0\\0\\3\end{array}\right], \left[\begin{array}{c}4\\3\\0\\2\end{array}\right], \left[\begin{array}{c}-3\\0\\1\\3\end{array}\right] \right\} \end{equation*}
  1. A basis because it both spans and is linearly independent.
  2. Spans, but not a basis as it is linearly dependent.
  3. Linearly indpendent, but not a basis as it fails to span.
  4. Fails to span and linearly independent, so not a basis.
