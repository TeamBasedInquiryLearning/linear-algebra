Activity 1.2.16.

Consider the matrix
\begin{equation*} B=\left[\begin{array}{ccc|c} 2 & 4 & 2 & -4 \\ -2 & -4 & 1 & 1 \\ 3 & 6 & -1 & -4 \end{array}\right]. \end{equation*}
which has the same terms as \(A\) from the previous activity.
Can \(\RREF(A)\) be used to find \(\RREF(B)\text{?}\)
  1. No, a new calculuation is required.
  2. Yes, \(\RREF(A)\) and \(\RREF(B)\) are exactly the same.
  3. Yes, \(\RREF(A)\) may be slightly modified to find \(\RREF(B)\text{.}\)
