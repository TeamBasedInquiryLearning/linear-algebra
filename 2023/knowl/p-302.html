\begin{equation*} x_1\left[\begin{array}{c}1\\-1\\0\end{array}\right]+ x_2\left[\begin{array}{c}-2\\0\\1\end{array}\right]+ x_3\left[\begin{array}{c}-2\\-2\\2\end{array}\right]=\vec{w} \end{equation*}
has...
  1. at least one solution for every vector \(\vec w\in\IR^3\text{.}\)
  2. at least one solution for some vector \(\vec w\in\IR^3\text{.}\)
  3. no solutions for every vector \(\vec w\in\IR^3\text{.}\)
  4. no solutions for some vector \(\vec w\in\IR^3\text{.}\)
