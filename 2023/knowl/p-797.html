Let \(T: \IR^n \rightarrow \IR^m\) be a linear map with standard matrix \(A\text{.}\) Determine whether each of the following statements means \(T\) is (A) injective, (B) surjective, or (C) bijective (both).
  1. The system of linear equations given by the augmented matrix \(\left[\begin{array}{c|c}A & \vec{b} \end{array}\right]\) has a solution for all \(\vec{b} \in \IR^m\text{.}\)
  2. The system of linear equations given by the augmented matrix \(\left[\begin{array}{c|c}A & \vec{b} \end{array}\right]\) has exactly one solution for all \(\vec{b} \in \IR^m\text{.}\)
  3. The system of linear equations given by the augmented matrix \(\left[\begin{array}{c|c} A & \vec{0} \end{array}\right]\) has exactly one solution.
