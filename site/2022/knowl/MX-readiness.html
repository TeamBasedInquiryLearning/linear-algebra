  2. Identify the domain and codomain of linear transformations.

    • Review: YouTube 2

    • Review: Section 3.2

  4. Determine if a linear transformation is injective and/or surjective.

    • Review: Section 3.4

  5. Interpret the ideas of injectivity and surjectivity in multiple ways.

    • Review: YouTube 3
