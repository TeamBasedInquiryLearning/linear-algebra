Circling our pivots, \(\left[\begin{array}{cccc} \circledNumber{1} & 1 & 0 & 3 \\ 0 & \circledNumber{1} & 1 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{array}\right]\) we see that each pivot is a 1, they are in a descending staircase pattern, and the row(s) of zeroes is at the bottom. However, there is not a zero above the second pivot, so it is not in reduced row echelon form.
